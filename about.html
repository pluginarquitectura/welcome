about plugin arquitectura

Together we are experiencing a whole new revolution where design and

production procceses in which new technology like digital fabrication

are getting more involved by the hour. In plugin arquitectura we are

commited to develop this new tools so you can experience your own custom

plugin arquitectura is and architecture and computational design office

based in Mexico City. With more than 8 years in experience from the conceptual

design to the execution planning. We also provide plugin services for external

architectural projects with the purpose to create a more efficent and comfortable
